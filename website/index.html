Our Mission

Cupertino is known as a bike-friendly community — but dangerous intersections, blocked bike lanes, broken sidewalks, and near-misses still go unreported every day. We're putting a student-built reporting tool directly into the community's hands, so no safety concern goes unnoticed.

📣

Report

Residents document hazards with a category, map pin, photo, and description — in under two minutes.

📊

Analyze

Students analyze reports for trends and clusters, building a Transportation Safety Report with clear recommendations.

🏛

Act

Verified, ranked hazards are presented to Cupertino city staff so the highest-priority issues get fixed first.
