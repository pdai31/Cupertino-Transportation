👥 Our Team

Six student teams, one mission: safer streets for Cupertino. Meet the people behind the project.

Advisor & Project Leadership

📣

Outreach & Media Team

Runs social media, designs educational content and infographics, conducts community interviews, and leads engagement efforts.

🚴

BikeFest Team

Handles logistics, activity design, route planning, and materials for the September 26 BikeFest scavenger hunt.

🎤

Presentation Team

Packages the project's findings into professional presentations and delivers updates to stakeholders and the city.
