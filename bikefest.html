🚴 Cupertino BikeFest Scavenger Hunt

Saturday, September 26, 2026 — An educational bike safety adventure across Cupertino. Ride, solve clues, learn safety skills, win prizes!

What is it?

Our team designed an educational scavenger hunt for Cupertino BikeFest. Participants of all ages bike a safe route through the city, completing challenges at each checkpoint — identifying safe bike infrastructure, practicing hand signals, and discovering key safety facts along the way.

It's transportation safety education that doesn't feel like a lecture. 🎉

The Route & Checkpoints

Five stops along a safe, bikeable loop — each with a challenge and a clue to the next stop.

1️⃣ Civic Center Plaza

Challenge: Helmet fit check & bike safety inspection. Get your scavenger hunt card!

2️⃣ Regnart Creek Trail

Challenge: Identify three features that make a trail crossing safe.

3️⃣ Library Field

Challenge: Hand signal relay — demonstrate left, right, and stop signals.

4️⃣ Memorial Park

Challenge: Spot-the-hazard photo game using real examples from our hazard map.

5️⃣ Don Burnett Bridge

Challenge: Safe Routes trivia — then ride back to the finish for prizes! 🏆
